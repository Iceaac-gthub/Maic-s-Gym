About Maic's GYM

Maic's Gym is fully equipped with top-of-the-line fitness brands such as HOIST, Hammer Strength, Cybex, Life Fitness, Technogym, and Star Trac, ensuring you have the best tools to achieve your goals.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include an indoor track, climbing wall, octagon/cage, two boxing rings, spinning room, aerobics area/dance floor, shower/locker/steam rooms for both men and women, cardio area, cross rig, snack bar, garden, and a spacious parking lot.

Location: 7 Ditchoy Street, Midtown Subdivision, Barangay San Roque, Marikina City, Philippines, 1801
